# KOS — Team Guide

Welcome to **KOS**, the whitelist & raffle platform for Web3 communities. This
guide walks your team through running raffles end to end.

**Dashboard:** https://kos-wl-bot-dashboard-3a8x.vercel.app
**Need help?** Email **Theonlyrealoutis@gmail.com** or DM **[@Tosincrypt](https://x.com/Tosincrypt)** on X — the developer behind KOS.

## 1. Sign in
Click **Continue with Discord**. Signing in creates your account automatically —
no passwords. Your access to a community is decided by your role in it.

## 2. Create your organization
Your **organization** is your community's space on KOS: its own dashboard, team,
branding and data (fully isolated from every other community). Set your logo,
banner and description under **Settings → Branding** (drag & drop an image or
paste a URL).

## 3. Connect your Discord server
**Settings → Discord servers**:
1. Click **Invite bot** to add the KOS bot to your server (you must be the
   **server owner** or have **Manage Server**).
2. Click **Connect**.

Once connected, every raffle in that server appears on your dashboard. You can
connect more than one server.

## 4. Host a raffle
Two ways:

- **From the dashboard:** **Raffles → + New raffle**. Pick the server + channel,
  set the project name, title (e.g. `GTD` / `FCFS`), WL spots, eligible roles,
  start/end time, ping and wallet chains, optionally drop a banner image, then
  **Create**. The bot posts it to Discord within a few seconds.
- **In Discord:** run **`/raffle`** in your server.

## 5. Track entries
Open a raffle to watch entries stream in live, search participants, and copy
usernames. The **Dashboard** tab shows live activity; **Analytics** shows trends,
top raffles and top hosts.

## 6. End & pick winners
A raffle ends at its end time automatically, or hit **End Now** on the raffle
page. Winners are drawn with a **verifiable, cryptographically-committed random
seed** — anyone can confirm the draw wasn't tampered with. Need a redo? **Reroll**
the whole pool or specific winners.

## 7. Collect winner wallets
Winners are prompted to submit a wallet for the raffle's chain(s). Find them all
under **Wallets** — copy addresses, or export to **CSV / Excel**. Each completed
raffle also has a downloadable **proof** (winner card + PDF + CSV) under
**Reports**.

## Team roles
Invite teammates under **Team** (by Discord ID or a shareable link) and assign:

| Role | Can do |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Everything, incl. transferring/deleting the org. |
| **Admin** | Everything except transfer/delete — run raffles, export wallets, manage members, branding. |
| **Moderator** | Run raffles day-to-day: create/edit/reroll/end + view data and export reports. |
| **Collab Manager** | For collab partners: create/edit raffles + view participants/analytics/reports. |
| **Viewer** | Read-only: participants, wallets, analytics, reports. |

## Tips
- Winners must add a wallet to appear in the address export.
- Use the **Blacklist** to keep known alts/farmers out.
- Scheduled raffles ping `@everyone` when they go live.
- Every completed raffle keeps a verifiable proof under **Reports**.
